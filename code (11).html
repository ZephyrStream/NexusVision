Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions, answered. Everything you need to know before you start.

Do I need a VPN?

While our service is secure, a VPN is highly recommended for two reasons: 1) It protects your privacy from your ISP. 2) It can bypass any ISP-level blocking to ensure a more stable connection, especially for live sports.

What devices are supported?

Our service works on virtually any device. This includes Smart TVs (Samsung, LG), Android TV Boxes, Amazon Firestick, Apple TV, MAG boxes, as well as iOS/Android smartphones and Windows/Mac computers. We provide access via M3U link, Xtream Codes API, and have recommended apps for each platform.

Is this service legal?

We provide access to publicly available streams from around the world. The legality can vary depending on your country's laws and how you use the service. We encourage all users to understand their local regulations and use a VPN for privacy.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of secure payment methods, including major credit cards and cryptocurrencies for enhanced anonymity. Please contact our support team for specific options available in your region.
